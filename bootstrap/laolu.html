Portfolio About us

We are a registered company under the Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria(Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990) 1st March 1991 with RC: 161860. We are a growing indigenous multi tasked resource company with background experience in construction, Real Estate Development and management, Global marketing and brokerage services.
We have staedily developed into a group with interests in Building Construction, Real Estate Development, Farming and Global Oil Brokerage services. Our team specializes in offering strategic services to organisations in both the public and private sector.
We focus on design and/or management of technology resources, with the aim of optimizing efficiency of manual and automated processes through the utilization of modern business concepts and resources. We achieve these by integrating these Technologies to position our clients to meet current management and business challenges, and also prepare them for the ever changing benchmarks and culture of service delivery.

Our Vision



To grow our position and reach as the premier integrated services provider that continually meets and exceeds clients expectations through the development and provision of state of the art solutions and services
To provide our clients with cuttting edge solutions in the most unique and socially responsible manner, while creating and delivering solutions that enrich our clients and business partners through efficiency and cost-saving.

Oil & Gas

We made our first entry into OIL & GAS sector by supplying Automotive Gas Oil (Diesel) for domestic use to various households and gradually moved up to the supply and importation of small cargoes, hence supplying some Majors in the down stream sector (Masters Energy, Phoenix Petroleum, Capital Oil Plc etc.) We went into supply of Black Oil for various industries both trucking and marine deliveries to storage. We buy over un-utilized LPO and refinance them to supply the industry. Our source for the product is from Refineries in Europe through direct importation.
